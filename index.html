What is Hyperbola?

Hyperbola is a mobile app built to empower small shopkeepers and customers in Tier-3 and smaller cities of India by offering a seamless online shopping experience.

The app enables local Kirana stores, cosmetic shops, garment stores, chai/snack shops, and more to list their products digitally and reach a wider customer base without complex logistics or tech overhead.
